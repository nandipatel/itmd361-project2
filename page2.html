Cities in India

Jaipur

Jaipur is a city in Rajasthan, founded in 1727. Also know as the Pink City. A city known for many things like the color of its building, tourist sites, and arts and craft.

Population: ~ 3.1 Million Places to visit in Jaipur:
  • Hawa Mahal
  • Amber Palace
  • Jal Mahal
  • Jaigarh Fort

Agra

Agra is a city in Uttar Pradesh (northern India), founded in the early 16th century. It is known for the Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders of the world. A palace that Shah Jahãn built for his wife, Mumtãz Mahal.

Population: ~ 1.6 Million

Places to visit in Agra:

  • Taj Mahal

  • Pearl Mosque

  • Tomb of Akbar the Great

  • Anguri Bagh

Gandhinagar

Gandhinagar is a city in Gujarat, western part of India. It is part of a dry state, meaning an alcohol free place. The city was named for Mahatma Gandhi.

Population: ~ 206, 167

Places to visit in Gandhinagar:

  • BAPS Akshardham Temple

  • Adalaj Stepwell

  • Swarnim Park

  • Salt Mount

Mumbai

Mumbai is a city in the Maharashtra. Mumbai was called Bombay, and is known as the 'City of Dreams'.

Population: ~ 18.4 Million

Places to visit in Mumbai:

  • Gateway of India

  • Film City

  • Elephanta Island

  • Wonders Park
